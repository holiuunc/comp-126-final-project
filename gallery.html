Events Recap!

FIRST GBM

We kicked off our first General Board Meeting by welcoming new and returning members with a fun game of Human Bingo! The perfect way to turn a room full of strangers into our KASA family!

KASA MT

MT is a two-night-three-day trip where KASA execs and member get to know each other. As part of the Gajok (family) program, we aim to tighten the bond between everyone through games, activities, and food.

KASA GALA

A night of cultural celebration, performances, and gathering. Our annual KASA Gala brings together students, alumni, and friends to honor Korean culture and showcase talent across campus.
