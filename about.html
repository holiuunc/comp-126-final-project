Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join KASA?

There are multiple ways to stay engaged with KASA! The easiest way to keep up with us is to follow our instagram (@unc_kasa) and join our GroupMe. Another way to become a member is to request membership at Heel Life.

Are there any fees?

You can pay a $7 yearly due to get perks like discounted events and merch, but you don’t necessarily have to pay dues to be an active participant. Just show up and have fun!

How often are meetings?

We typically hold general body meetings (GBMs) once or twice a month plus fun collabs and socials throughout the semester! Look out for our instagram and groupme for upcoming events!
